Features

Here are some of the features of Vinted Bot

Custom Vinted tracking

With the /sub command, you can subscribe to a specific keyword with the desired filters. When a new listing with that keyword or/and the selected filters is posted, you will be notified in a Discord channel.

Views booster

With the /views command, you can boost the views of a listing. The bot will automatically give the listing the desired number of views.

Advanced user info

By clicking the "Seller info" button on any notification, you will receive a message that includes a trust rating and other information about the user.

Analytics

With the /analytics command, you can get a detailed report about the price of items in your research.
